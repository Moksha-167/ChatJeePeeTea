Sustainable Agricultural Practices

There are many ways farmers can make their farms more sustainable. A few techniques include:

  • Crop Rotation: Changing the types of crops grown to make the soil better and keep pests away.
  • Agroforestry: Planting trees and bushes along with crops and animals to boost biodiversity
  • Organic Farming: Using natural ways to fertilize and control pests. Drip Irrigation: Making sure water is used wisely.
  • Cover Cropping: Planting certain crops to stop soil from washing away.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): Mixing different ways to control pests, like biological, cultural, and mechanical methods.
