Sustainable Agriculture



Why is Sustainable Agricultural Neccesary?

Sustainable farming is crucial for a multiple of reasons such as saving water, reducing pollution from chemical fertilizers and pesticides, and preventing soil from eroding. This method of farming helps the farmers conserve resources therefore they can save their money on supplies. Additionally, it makes sure that these resources are available for all future generations. Farming practices such as composting, crop-rotation and cover cropping helps in trapping carbon emissions in the soil, removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. Optimizing the use of resources also ensures that agriculture can meet the rising demand for food due to the growing population. It may even lead to higher crop yields because the sustainable methods will not degrade the lands as compared to conventional methods.
Sustainable farming is important because:

  • Environmental Protection: It helps stop soil from washing away, saves water, and reduces pollution from chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Economic Viability: Using sustainable methods keeps farming profitable for a long time and helps farmers not rely too much on pricey supplies.

  • Climate Change Mitigation: Sustainable methods trap carbon in the soil and lessen greenhouse gas emissions.
