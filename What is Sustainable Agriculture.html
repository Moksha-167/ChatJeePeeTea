Sustainable Agriculture



What is Sustainable Agriculture

Sustainable agriculture is a way to ensure that farming, one of the biggest and most important industries, runs sustainability in the most efficient way. To ensure sustainable agriculture is achieved many people have to pitch in and work together as the agricultural industry not only includes the farmers but also the food processors, distributors, retailers, consumers and waste managers. All the people apart from sustainable agriculture dont just have to implement solutions but also have to negotiate and compete against others in the industry.
